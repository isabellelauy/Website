Scotia Empower

Guiding newcomers to Canada through the Canadian financial system.

Role

Designer

Timeline

August 26, 2023

Team

Elena Xu, Sheldon Lewis, Wyeth Shiu

Problem Statement

Create a Technology Focused Solution Championing Sustainability and Client Empowerment in Banking Operations and Customer Experiences for Every Future.

Approach

21% of Canadians are newcomers to Canada and 17% are still unsatisfied by current financial solutions for immigrants and newcomers. We saw an unmet need for 1,428,000 Canadians to receive better financial advice at their own pace.

First, we sought existing products/programs for new comers offered by Scotiabank. Scotiabank has programs like the StartRIght, New Comer program and the International Student program, but finding these programs was difficult and learning more about the specific benefits was confusing.

So, we gathered data by talking to our families. Our main objective was to relieve pain points that immigrants encounter when opening their first bank account in Canada. We also wanted to know if our families would be able to find the New Comer program homepage.

After briefly talking to our families and friends, we narrowed our focus into two areas for potential improvement for the Scotiabank website.

1. Lack of experience banking with the Canadian financial system
