Scotia Empower

Guiding newcomers to Canada through the Canadian financial system.

Role

Designer

Timeline

August 26, 2023

Team

Elena Xu, Sheldon Lewis, Wyeth Shiu

Problem Statement

Create a Technology Focused Solution Championing Sustainability and Client Empowerment in Banking Operations and Customer Experiences for Every Future.

Approach

21% of Canadians are newcomers to Canada and 17% are still unsatisfied by current financial solutions for immigrants and newcomers. We saw an unmet need for 1,428,000 Canadians to receive better financial advice at their own pace.

First, we sought existing products/programs for new comers offered by Scotiabank. Scotiabank has programs like the StartRIght, New Comer program and the International Student program, but finding these programs was difficult and learning more about the specific benefits was confusing.

So, we gathered data by talking to our families. Our main objective was to relieve pain points that immigrants encounter when opening their first bank account in Canada. We also wanted to know if our families would be able to find the New Comer program homepage.

After briefly talking to our families and friends, we narrowed our focus into two areas for potential improvement for the Scotiabank website.

1. Lack of experience banking with the Canadian financial system

2. Difficult to increase financial literacy on Scotiabank‚Äôs website due to promotion and advertisements

Design

We focused on two areas for our design:

1. Increasing accessibility

2. Promoting sustainability

Increasing Accessibiliy

We learned that a majority of users were unfamiliar with commonly-used financial terms featured on banking websites, and it was hard to find clear definitions and explanations. We wanted to display these explanations in a convenient way, so we created the Learning Tool. The Learning Tool provides educational widgets with optional articles and embedded definition pop-ups.

Increasing Sustainability

How can we inform our users of existing Scotia programs/products for new comers? We‚Äôve added a survey to personalize the order of recommended products that would inform the user of programs/products without confusing promotions or advertisements.

Outcome

With Scotia Empower, we can improve the quantity and quality of visibility of the products and services. Customers will be connected with the proper products and increase the economic sustainability of our customers by educating them of the commonly-used terms in Canadian banking system.
